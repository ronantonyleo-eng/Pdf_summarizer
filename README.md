## Features

### Core Capabilities
- ** Multi-Format Support** - Upload PDF (text & scanned), TXT files
- ** OCR Technology** - Reads text from scanned documents and images
- ** AI-Powered Summarization** - Extracts key information intelligently
- ** Structure Detection** - Automatically identifies headings and sections
- ** Three View Modes** - Summary, Structured View, Key Points
- ** Export Options** - Copy to clipboard or download as TXT

### User Experience
- ** Clean, Modern UI** - Professional interface
- ** Real-time Progress** - Multi-stage progress tracking
- ** Fully Responsive** - Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile
- ** Fast Processing** - Client-side processing for privacy
- ** 100% Private** - Files never leave your browser

## Quick Start

### Try It Now
1. Visit the [live demo](https://ronantonyleo-eng.github.io/Pdf_summarizer)
2. Drag & drop a PDF or TXT file
3. Wait for AI processing
4. Get your organized summary!
